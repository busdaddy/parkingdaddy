FAQ

Common questions.

When are you launching?

Very soon — we're putting the final pieces in place. Join the waitlist and you'll be one of the first to know the day bookings open.

Who's driving my car? Are they insured?

Every Parking Daddy driver is licensed, background-checked, and covered by our insurance policy while your vehicle is in our care. We treat each car like our own — and we don't take chances with who we hire.

What if I can't be there for key pickup?

That's the whole point — you don't have to be. Drop your key in a lockbox, leave it with your doorman, hide it somewhere we agree on, or use remote unlock if your car supports it (Tesla, newer BMWs, etc.). You tell us how during booking.

What areas do you cover?

At launch we'll serve ZIP 94123 only — the Marina District plus parts of Cow Hollow and Pacific Heights. We're planning to expand to other SF neighborhoods after. Want yours next? Tell us via the Contact page.

Where do you park my car?

We relocate your car to the best available legal spot in the area. Sometimes that's close by, sometimes it's a little further depending on what's open — either way, we'll text you exactly where it is so you always know where to find it.

How do you avoid getting a ticket?

We move your car ahead of the posted sweep time and keep it in a legal spot until the coast is clear. Knowing the neighborhood — which blocks get swept when — is a big part of how we keep your car ticket-free.

What if I need to cancel?

Cancel free up to 2 hours before your scheduled move. Within 2 hours, we charge a small dispatch fee since we've already routed a driver. See our Terms for the full policy.

Do you operate on weekends?

We work around the posted street-sweeping schedule for your block — whenever your sweep happens, that's when we move your car. Book it the same way regardless of the day.

How do I pay?

Card payment at booking via Stripe. We charge when you tap Confirm & Pay — no surprise fees later.
