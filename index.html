FAQ

Common questions.

What if I can't be there for key pickup?

That's the whole point — you don't have to be. Drop your key in a lockbox, leave it with your doorman, hide it somewhere we agree on, or use remote unlock if your car supports it (Tesla, newer BMWs, etc.). You tell us how during booking.

What areas do you cover?

Right now we serve ZIP 94123 only — that's the Marina District plus parts of Cow Hollow and Pacific Heights. We're expanding to other SF neighborhoods soon. Want yours next? Tell us via the Contact page.

What if the original spot is taken when you return my car?

We return your car to the nearest legal spot on the same block or one block over. You'll get a text with the exact location and a photo before we leave.

How do you avoid getting a ticket?

We move your car at least 15 minutes before the official sweep start time and only return it after the posted window ends. We also know which blocks the sweepers actually hit on time vs. skip — local knowledge helps.

Is my car insured while you have it?

Our drivers are licensed and we're working on commercial coverage for this phase of the business. For full peace of mind during our pilot phase, your own insurance policy is still the primary coverage. We're happy to talk through this — reach out via Contact.

What if I need to cancel?

Cancel free up to 4 hours before your scheduled move. Within 4 hours, we charge a $20 dispatch fee since we've already routed a driver. See our Terms for the full policy.

Do you operate on weekends?

SF street sweeping is mostly Mon–Fri, so that's when we operate (6am–2pm). A few blocks have weekend schedules — book those the same way.

How do I pay?

Card payment at booking via Stripe. We charge when you tap Confirm & Pay — no surprise fees later.
